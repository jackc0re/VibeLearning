## Question 2
Binary tree max children per node?

A) 1  
B) 2  
C) 3  
D) Unlimited

<details>
<summary>Click for answer</summary>

**B) 2**

Binary means at most 2 children (left, right).

</details>

## Question 3
Inorder traversal order?

A) Root, Left, Right  
B) Left, Root, Right  
C) Left, Right, Root  
D) Right, Root, Left

<details>
<summary>Click for answer</summary>

**B) Left, Root, Right**

Inorder visits left subtree, then node, then right.

</details>

## Question 4
BST search time (balanced)?

A) O(1)  
B) O(log n)  
C) O(n)  
D) O(n¬≤)

<details>
<summary>Click for answer</summary>

**B) O(log n)**

Each comparison eliminates half the tree.

</details>

## Question 5
BST property?

A) Left > Root > Right  
B) Left < Root < Right  
C) All values equal  
D) Random order

<details>
<summary>Click for answer</summary>

**B) Left < Root < Right**

All left descendants are smaller, all right are larger.

</details>
