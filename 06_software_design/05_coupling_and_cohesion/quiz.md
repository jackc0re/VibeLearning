## Question 3
Which combination is usually best?

- A) High coupling, low cohesion
- B) Low coupling, high cohesion
- C) High coupling, high cohesion
- D) Low coupling, low cohesion

<details>

## Question 4
What is a common sign of high coupling?

- A) You can change one module without touching others
- B) A small change forces edits across many files
- C) Functions have clear names
- D) Tests are fast

<details>
<summary>Show Answer</summary>

**B)** High coupling increases the blast radius of change.

</details>

## Question 5

- A) Hard-code dependencies everywhere
- B) Use dependency injection (pass dependencies as parameters)
- C) Put everything in global variables
- D) Avoid naming things

<details>

**B)** Passing dependencies makes components easier to replace and test.
